Adopt Zero Trust for Enhanced Security

Adopt Zero Trust for Enhanced Security Your company grants multiple users access to its resources. Despite varying needs, everyone requires some level of access to important data. Managing these connections and resources can be quite intricate. Shifting to a hybrid, multicloud setup means your resources are spread across different IT environments, including IoT devices, each with different visibility and control levels. It's challenging to ensure the right people have appropriate access to the correct data. To make informed decisions, you need context.

Another significant concern is the rise in malicious activities like malware, ransomware, and phishing, posing threats to your network, digital assets, and business. According to the latest report on data breach costs, expenses have surged by 15% in the last three years.

Arrange a consultation with CAPTIV X-Force to discuss the report's findings

CAPTIV's zero trust security approach can enhance your cybersecurity and manage risks in today's complex business landscape. This strategy allows users to securely connect with sensitive data, ensuring they have the right access at the right time under the right conditions while safeguarding your organization from cyber threats. It facilitates the creation of zero trust policies, enabling secure remote and hybrid work, as well as maintaining a high level of security for on-premises work behind your firewall.
